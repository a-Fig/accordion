# Task starter — Email

Use the Accordion brand voice (see `system-prompt.md`).

Structure
- **Subject line** — ≤ 6 words, specific, no clickbait. (e.g. "Folding is on by default")
- **Preheader** — one short sentence.
- **Body** — short paragraphs, observable facts, one clear point per message. No hype.
- **CTA** — one primary action, plainly named.
- **Sign-off** — plain and human ("— The Accordion team"). No "Cheers!", no exclamation stacks.

Rules
- Transactional emails state exactly what happened and what (if anything) the reader should do.
- Marketing emails make one claim the reader can verify in the product.
- Forbidden words: powerful, seamless, leverage, AI-powered, intelligent, streamlined.
