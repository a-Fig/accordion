# Task starter — Landing page hero

Use the Accordion brand voice (see `system-prompt.md`). Produce hero copy:

1. **Headline** — ≤ 7 words, a specific claim or tension. (e.g. "Fold context. Keep momentum.")
2. **Subheadline** — 1–2 sentences stating what the product does, observably.
3. **Primary CTA** — 2–4 words (e.g. "Download for desktop").
4. **Secondary CTA** — optional (e.g. "Read the docs").

Rules
- No hype words (powerful, seamless, leverage, AI-powered, intelligent, streamlined).
- The subheadline must describe a real, visible behavior — what the user sees happen.
- May use the tagline "Your session, intact." as headline or kicker, unaltered.

Reference
- Headline: "Your session, intact."
  Sub: "Long AI sessions break when old context falls out of the window. Accordion folds it instead of deleting it — recessed, reversible, never gone."
  CTA: "Download for desktop" · "See how folding works"
