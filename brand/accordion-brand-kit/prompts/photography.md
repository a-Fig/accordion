# Task starter — Brand photography (gpt-image-2 / image models)

Generate Accordion-brand images. Default engine: **gpt-image-2**, quality medium.

## Master prompt template
> Documentary 35mm photograph of [SUBJECT], [SETTING] at night. Practical light only —
> monitor glow / desk lamp / window light — in a near-monochrome room; let a soft screen
> glow be the only color. A real lived-in workspace (mug, scattered notes, mechanical keyboard).
> Film grain, natural soft shadows, slightly imperfect, low-key cinematic. Subject looking at
> the work, not the camera. No legible text on screen, no logos, no watermark.

## Cast & diversity (required)
Name a specific ethnicity per prompt and vary it across a set — e.g. Black, East Asian, South
Asian, Latina, Middle Eastern, mixed-race. Vary body types, not just skin tone. If only one person
appears, make a deliberate choice — don't default to a light-skinned subject.

## What to AVOID in the prompt
studio strobes, ring light, stock-photo terms, "professional," "premium," "engineers at laptops,"
glass coworking spaces, teams pointing at monitors, handshakes, perfect symmetry, gradient
backgrounds, floating objects on white, AI-smooth skin/surfaces, neon cyberpunk.

## Banned cliché concepts
hourglasses, lightbulbs, glowing brains, network nodes, robots, holographic UI, "AI" blue swirls,
circuit-board textures, magnifying glasses over data.

## Subject substitutes for "a person doing X"
hands on a mechanical keyboard; an over-the-shoulder view of a screen; a desk flat-lay; a laptop
on a real desk; a phone in hand. The screen may show an abstract dark UI with small teal/purple/
orange/blue blocks (the context grid) — never readable text.

## Quality bar
film grain present; documentary framing; natural imperfection; if it looks fake or stocky, regenerate.

## No-text guardrail (append verbatim)
> No text, no captions, no UI labels, no logos, no watermark anywhere in the image.

Never name a real publication or brand in the prompt.
